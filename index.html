01 — Features
Support That Knows Your Voice
Every interaction feels like your brand. Every question finds an answer. Every user feels welcomed.
T

Ticket System

Create, track, and resolve support tickets with full conversation history. Agents auto-triage by urgency and topic. Escalation paths route to the right team member. Nothing falls through the cracks.

S

Article Search

Semantic search across your entire knowledge base. Agents surface the most relevant articles before a ticket is even created. Users find answers in seconds, not minutes. Self-service that actually works.

H

System Status

Real-time health monitoring across all connected products. Incident timelines, uptime percentages, and proactive notifications. Your users know what is happening before they need to ask.

O

Onboarding Progress

Guided tours that walk new users through every feature. Step-by-step checklists with progress tracking. Contextual help that appears exactly when needed. Turn signups into power users.
